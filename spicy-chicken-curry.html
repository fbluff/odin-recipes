Spicy Chicken Curry

Description

Negeri Sembilan (a Malaysian state) is known as home of hot and spicy dishes and chicken curry is one of them. There is no garlic or onion. Just chicken, cooked in fresh coconut milk and blended with turmeric along with bird's eye chilies.

Ingredients
  • 800g chicken, with skin and bones, cut in 5 pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tumeric powder
  • 20 bird's eye chilies
  • 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, white part only
  • 200g fresh coconut milk
  • 1 dried tamarind piece
  • 300g baby potatoes, cut in quarters
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
Instructions
  1. Blend the bird's eye chilies.

  2. In a pot, place a cup of water and soak the chicken and potatoes with blended chilies under low heat. Let it sit for 5 minutes.

  3. Add coconut milk, lemongrass, tamarind piece, salt and stir them altogether.

  4. Close the pot and let the chicken cooked for about 10 minutes
