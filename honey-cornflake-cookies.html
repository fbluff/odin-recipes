Honey Cornflakes Cookies

Description

Honey cornflakes cookies are so easy to prepare making it great for parties and the festive seasons. Decorations are also made easy with just some crushed nuts or chopped Maraschino cherries.

Ingredients
  • 30g unstalked butter
  • 30g honey
  • 20g cornflakes
  • Chopped maraschino cherries
Instructions
  1. Melt butter over medium heat.

  2. When butter melts, add honey and mix well.

  3. Remove pan from heat and add cornflakes to the mixture.

  4. Place the cornflakes into mini cupcake moulds.

  5. Decorate with a small piece of maraschono cherry.

  6. Allow to cool before serving.
